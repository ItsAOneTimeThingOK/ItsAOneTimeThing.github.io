What does this app do?

This app helps cosplayers and costume makers easily organize their costume making, help them find new tips and tricks and meet likeminded people also passionate about cosplay.

How the app works

You can write down and then divide the costume into parts (Hat, tail, jacket, pants and so on) Then you can make a list of things you need for it. You can also add pictures of each costume part or just the costume itself. The way to search for materials or props online is to just tick the details (colour, size, shape, material etc)
So for example you need a white pirate shirt you just tick white, long-sleeved etc.
