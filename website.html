Summary of the app

My app is made to help cosplayers or costume makers easily organize their costume making journey and also help them search for items.
The problem I am solving is something I face quite often myself. It is super hard to start working on a costume and even harder to search for the right materials to make it with. So this app will make it a lot easier by helping you make a plan for it and at the same time help you find the items online (from aliexpress/wish/ebay etc)

Who this app is for

Mostly this app would be used by, as i said people who like making costumes and people who like just buying the parts separately. It can also be used to put together other more complicated outfits.

How the app works

You can write down and then divide the costume into parts (Hat, tail, jacket, pants and so on) Then you can make a list of things you need for it. You can also add pictures of each costume part or just the costume itself. The way to search for materials or props online is to just tick the details (colour, size, shape, material etc)
So for example you need a white pirate shirt you just tick white, long-sleeved etc.

App community

There was also an idea that there could be a part where people could rate cosplayers and can socialize with each other. Cosplayers can also share fully made drafts with links used parts/materials and tutorials they used to make the costume. That way it is easier for people who aren't good at making plans or don't have the time to do them start with their costume. You can also for example upload pictures of the clothes or materials that you ordered online to show others if that source is trustable or not.
